Common red flags this UI illustrates

Every item below is intentionally reproduced for educational discussion.

  • Unrealistic earnings.
    Promised "daily income" that beats any legitimate investment by orders of magnitude.
  • Pressure to deposit.
    VIP upgrade screens that push users to recharge before they can earn more.
  • Referral-based growth.
    Multi-level commissions reward existing users for recruiting new ones.
  • Fake VIP upgrades.
    Higher levels lock more tasks behind larger deposits to manufacture urgency.
  • Withdrawal problems.
    "Pending review", "frozen", and "minimum balance" are classic stalling tactics.
  • Brand impersonation.
    Real scams often borrow trusted brand names. This demo intentionally uses none.
