Your videos, your library

A clean, private video library for the things you actually watch.

VidLog is a small personal-media app: log the videos you watch, track your progress, take notes, and pick what to watch next. No ads, no tiers, no money - just your library on your account.

What VidLog does

A small set of features that all work together.

🎬

Library

Browse the videos in your library, with thumbnails, duration and tags.

▶

Watch tracking

Mark videos as watched, see progress, and resume where you left off.

📝

Notes

Add a short note per video so you remember why you saved it.

🔔

Announcements

Lightweight messages from the admin (release notes, tips).

⚙️

Account settings

Update your display name, change your password, or delete your account.

🔒

Real authentication

PBKDF2-hashed passwords, signed session cookies, Cloudflare Workers + D1.

About this project

VidLog is a small full-stack project: a static frontend served from Cloudflare Pages assets, a Workers API for auth and data, and a D1 database for persistence. The frontend talks to the API at /api/*, with session cookies set by the worker. Source is available in the repository README.
